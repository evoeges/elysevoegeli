problem

How can we use design research methodologies to understand trust between people and their messaging apps?

solution

Read the full research findings, Trust Through Trickery at Common Place.

process

Inspired by research into dark patterns (manipulative design decisions to trick users), we want to understand if there are design patterns that foster trust between users and messaging apps- even when those apps are insecure. We're especially interested in these trust patterns with journalists, who rely on these apps for extremely confidential information.

We're looking into both the cognitive processes involved in trust patterns (such as the language used), and the physical aesthetics (such as color). Our findings are drawn from quantitative survey results, qualitative interviews, and current research in psychology and UI/UX design.
